04 — Logo
Logo Usage
The CWB Construction logo will be added here once finalized. Below are the placement guidelines and usage rules.

Logo will be placed here

Primary horizontal lockup — Green background version

Do

  • Use on dark green backgrounds with white + gold text
  • Maintain clear space around the logo (min 1x icon height)
  • Use the horizontal lockup for headers and business cards
  • Keep "CWB" in gold and "CONSTRUCTION" in white on dark
  • Use the icon mark alone as a favicon or app icon

Don't

  • Stretch, rotate, or distort the logo
  • Change the logo colors outside approved palette
  • Place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Add drop shadows, outlines, or effects
  • Use the icon without the wordmark on large formats
05 — Brand Voice
Tone & Messaging
CWB Construction's voice is confident, honest, and approachable. We speak directly and clearly — no jargon, no overselling.

We Sound Like

  • ✓ Confident but not arrogant
  • ✓ Honest and straightforward
  • ✓ Professional yet approachable
  • ✓ Knowledgeable without being technical
  • ✓ Warm but businesslike

We Don't Sound Like

  • ✗ Salesy or pushy
  • ✗ Overly casual or slangy
  • ✗ Vague or full of jargon
  • ✗ Making promises we can't keep
  • ✗ Cold or corporate

Key Phrases

Quality Craftsmanship Reliable Service Licensed & Insured No Surprises Built on Trust Delivered with Excellence 24/7 Emergency Response
